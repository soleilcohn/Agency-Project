Letter of Understanding

The Kia Foundation is a nonprofit organization that helps families who are struggling financially meet the cost of their pet care needs. Their motto is: "preventing animals from being in crisis by strengthening the common bond between owner and pet through assistance and education."

The Kia Foundation approaches every situation with a single goal: to make sure that every pet and their family has a strong, lasting bond that can survive even when the cost of healthcare, food, and supplies for the pet becomes a financial burden. The foundation strives to ensure that people do not have to surrender their pets to a shelter when a crisis strikes.

The Kia Foundation does a great deal for the people of Central New York. Their services include hosting monthly mobile pet food and supply giveaways, providing a meal delivery membership service, operating a pet thrift shop, and maintaining a pet food pantry. All of these services allow families to provide for their pet no matter the circumstance.

Sam Washington, the founder of The Kia Foundation, has a unique story for why he started the organization. Sam had a dog by the name of Kia. She was a 16 year old Shar Pei, Pitbull mix. In 2009, the Washington family was struggling financially and Kia's vet bills were simply too costly. Sam searched for a solution, but there was no organization out there that helped families in his specific situation. That is when Sam got the idea for The Kia Foundation.

Since The Kia Foundation is such a unique organization, we were immediately drawn to support it. We want to do everything we can to make the foundation well known to all people in Central New York. We reached out to Sam and offered to make fliers to put on campus and in surrounding neighborhoods, as well as help create graphics to increase social media following. Our goal is to increase awareness of the foundation's mission and expand its reach within the community.
