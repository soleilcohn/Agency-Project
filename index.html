Purpose of the Project

The purpose of this project is for our WRT 307 team to support the Kia Foundation in Syracuse, NY. As a team, we offered to make fliers to put on campus and in surrounding neighborhoods, as well as help create graphics to increase social media following. Our goal is to increase awareness of the foundation's mission and expand its reach within the community.

This Letter of Understanding outlines the expectations for the collaboration between the Kia Foundation and our WRT 307 team at Syracuse University. Our goal is to strengthen the foundation's outreach by developing materials that help increase visibility, engagement, and potential donations while aligning with the organization's mission.

Agency Overview

The Kia Foundation is a nonprofit organization that helps families who are struggling financially meet the cost of their pet care needs. Their motto is: "preventing animals from being in crisis by strengthening the common bond between owner and pet through assistance and education." The Kia Foundation approaches every situation with a single goal: to make sure that every pet and their family has a strong, lasting bond that can survive even when the cost of healthcare, food, and supplies for the pet becomes a financial burden. The foundation strives to ensure that people don't have to surrender their pets to a shelter when a crisis strikes. They aim to support families in providing for their pets during times of crisis.

The Kia Foundation does a lot for the people of Central New York to help them provide for their pets. Their services include hosting monthly mobile pet food and supply giveaways, providing a meal delivery membership service, operating a pet thrift shop, and maintaining a pet food pantry. All of these services allow for families to provide for their pet no matter the circumstance.

Sam Washington, the founder of The Kia Foundation, has a unique story for why he started the organization. Sam had a dog by the name of Kia — a 16-year-old Shar Pei/Pitbull mix. In 2009, the Washington family was struggling financially and Kia's vet bills were simply too costly. Sam searched for a solution, but there was no organization out there that helped families in his specific situation. That is when Sam got the idea for The Kia Foundation.

Responsibilities

Our WRT 307 Team Will:

  • Coordinate within our team to divide tasks across research, writing, and design so work is completed efficiently
  • Stay in regular communication with the Kia Foundation to clarify expectations and adjust based on feedback
  • Develop social media content and promotional materials that reflect the foundation's mission and audience
  • Revise our work based on feedback to ensure the final materials are clear, engaging, and effective
  • Submit all drafts and final deliverables on time

The Kia Foundation Will:

  • Provide feedback on drafts so our team can make revisions and improve the final materials
  • Share relevant information, images, or examples that will help guide our content and design choices
  • Communicate any preferences, priorities, or goals so our work aligns with the organization's needs
